Customised Digital Food Labels

In today’s modern era, being health conscious is more important than ever. With hundreds of different dietary requirements, and many different allergies. It is more relevant than ever to know what’s in your food. Currently, the food industry does this by providing the consumer with the required information, the way in which they display this information is not regulated, and often this is where consumers can be misled. Nutri aims to solve this problem!
